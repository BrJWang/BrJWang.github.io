About Calsol

Calsol, or the UC Berkeley Solar Vehicle Team, is a team of undergraduate engineers who build solar-powered vehicles to compete in various national competitions like the Formula Sun Grand Prix and the American Solar Challenge. We make a new vehicle every 2 years, and are currently designing the 11th generation vehicle set to race in the Summer of 2026.
